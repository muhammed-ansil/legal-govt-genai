Aadhaar is a 12-digit unique identity number issued by the Government of India.

To apply for Aadhaar, a person must visit an authorized Aadhaar enrollment center.

Basic steps:
1. Visit the nearest Aadhaar enrollment center.
2. Carry proof of identity and proof of address documents.
3. Provide biometric details such as fingerprints and iris scan.
4. After enrollment, an acknowledgment slip is given.
5. Aadhaar number is generated and sent by post or available online.

Aadhaar enrollment is free of cost.
