PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a unique identification number issued by the Income Tax Department of India.

PAN is required for income tax filing, opening bank accounts, and financial transactions.

Steps to apply for PAN:
1. Apply online through the official NSDL or UTIITSL website.
2. Fill the PAN application form with correct details.
3. Submit proof of identity, address, and date of birth.
4. Pay the applicable fee.
5. PAN card is issued after verification.

PAN application status can be tracked online.

This information is for awareness purposes only.
