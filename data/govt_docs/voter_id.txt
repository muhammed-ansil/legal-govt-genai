Voter ID is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India.

It allows citizens to vote in elections.

Steps to apply for Voter ID:
1. Apply online through the National Voters' Service Portal or offline at election offices.
2. Fill Form 6 with personal details.
3. Submit age and address proof.
4. Verification may be conducted by officials.
5. Voter ID is issued after approval.

Only eligible Indian citizens above 18 years can apply.

This information is for general awareness only.
